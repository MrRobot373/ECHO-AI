# Car Information — Echo AI

## Range
On a full charge the car has an estimated range of about 450 kilometres in city driving. Highway range is lower — typically 360 to 380 kilometres at speeds above 100 km/h. Range varies with air conditioning use, driving mode, and ambient temperature.

## Charging
The car supports both AC home charging and DC fast charging. A full charge at home using the 7.2 kW onboard charger takes approximately 6 hours. Using a 50 kW DC fast charger, you can reach 80 percent charge in around 35 minutes. Always unplug before driving and avoid leaving the battery below 10 percent for extended periods.

## Tyre Pressure
Recommended tyre pressure is 33 PSI for the front tyres and 35 PSI for the rear tyres. Check pressure when the tyres are cold — at least 3 hours after driving. Incorrect pressure affects range, handling, and tyre wear. A tyre pressure warning light will appear on the dashboard if pressure drops more than 25 percent below recommended levels.

## Service Schedule
The car should be serviced every 15,000 kilometres or once a year, whichever comes first. The service includes brake inspection, tyre rotation, cabin air filter replacement, and software updates. Battery health is checked at every service. Brake fluid should be replaced every 2 years regardless of mileage.

## Warranty
The vehicle is covered by a 3-year or 100,000 kilometre manufacturer warranty, whichever comes first. The high-voltage battery carries a separate 8-year or 160,000 kilometre warranty. Warranty does not cover damage from accidents, misuse, or unauthorised modifications.

## Driving Modes
Three driving modes are available. Eco mode limits power output to maximise range and is ideal for city commuting. Normal mode offers a balance of performance and efficiency for everyday driving. Sport mode unlocks full motor power and sharpens throttle response, but reduces range by 15 to 20 percent. Mode selection is available via the centre console or by voice command.

## Regenerative Braking
The car uses regenerative braking to recover energy when slowing down. Three levels are available: Low, Medium, and High. On High, the car decelerates significantly when you lift off the accelerator — this is called one-pedal driving. Regenerative braking extends range by up to 15 percent in stop-and-go traffic.

## Boot Space
The boot provides 350 litres of space with all seats up. Folding the rear seats flat increases capacity to 1,100 litres. The car also has a front storage compartment of 30 litres, useful for charging cables and small items.

## App Connectivity
The car connects to the companion mobile app over Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. From the app you can pre-condition the cabin temperature, schedule charging, check battery level remotely, and lock or unlock the doors. Make sure your phone is connected to the same Wi-Fi network or has Bluetooth enabled within 10 metres of the car.

## AC and Climate
The air conditioning system can be pre-conditioned remotely before you enter the vehicle to preserve driving range. Set your preferred temperature in the companion app. The car supports both cooling and heating. Using the heater in winter can reduce range by up to 20 percent — using seat heaters instead is more efficient.

## Warning Lights
A red battery icon means the high-voltage battery has a fault — stop safely and contact service. An orange turtle icon means the car is in reduced-power mode due to low battery or overheating. A blue snowflake means the battery is too cold for full performance. A tyre pressure warning means one or more tyres are significantly under-inflated.

## Software Updates
The car receives over-the-air software updates automatically when connected to Wi-Fi. Updates may add new features, improve range estimates, or fix minor issues. Installation typically takes 20 to 45 minutes. Do not start driving while an installation is in progress.

## Range Optimisation Tips
To maximise range: drive in Eco mode, keep speed below 90 km/h on highways, use regenerative braking on High, pre-condition the cabin while plugged in, and avoid rapid acceleration. Correct tyre pressure and removing unnecessary roof racks also helps. Cold weather reduces range — plan longer journeys with a charging stop if temperatures are below 10 degrees Celsius.

For service appointments call your nearest service centre or book through the companion app. In case of an accident involving the high-voltage battery, keep bystanders at least 10 metres away and call emergency services — do not attempt to open the battery casing.

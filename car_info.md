## Range
On a full charge the car has an estimated range of about 450 kilometres in
city driving. Highway range is lower at higher speeds.

## Charging
A standard home charger fills the battery from 20 to 80 percent in roughly six
hours. A DC fast charger does the same in about 35 minutes.

## Tyre pressure
Recommended tyre pressure is 33 PSI for the front tyres and 35 PSI for the rear
when cold. Check pressure monthly and before long trips.

## Service
The car is due for service every 15,000 kilometres or once a year, whichever
comes first. The dashboard shows a reminder when service is near.

## Warranty
The vehicle comes with a three year or 100,000 kilometre warranty, and an
eight year warranty on the battery pack.

## Driving modes
There are three driving modes: Eco for maximum range, Normal for everyday
driving, and Sport for quicker acceleration.

## Boot space
The boot holds about 350 litres, expandable to 1,100 litres with the rear seats
folded down. There is a small front storage compartment as well.
